The Best Movies According to Yuxiang

My Top Three Movies of All Time

Avatar

This is my favourite sci-fi movie. The CGI was exquisitely done, the script was beautifully written.

The Last Dance

This is my recent favourite movie which was made in Hong Kong. The story was about friendship, family relationship and life itself. It was very moving and enlightening at the same time.

The 300

The movie portrays the sacrifice everyone in a tightknit team is willing to make for each other. In real life, too many people have only "I"s in their volcabulary.
